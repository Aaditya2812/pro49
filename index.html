CAMEL-

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CAMEL:-

1)Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.

2)Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

3)Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.

4)Mother camels carry their calves up to 14 months before giving birth.
